Hiker's Weather Companion

This project is a concept for an app that helps hikers plan their adventures by providing real-time weather updates and trail conditions. It aims to ensure safety and enhance the hiking experience.

Features

  • Weather forecasts specific to trails
  • Emergency alerts and safety tips
  • Customizable hiking itinerary planner
